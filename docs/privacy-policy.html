Privacy Policy

How AquaTrack handles your data.

Last updated: June 19, 2026

Overview

AquaTrack is a daily water tracking app that helps you record cups of water, view daily progress, and follow a hydration streak. This Privacy Policy explains what information is stored and how it is handled.

Local device storage

AquaTrack stores app data locally on your device. Data is stored locally on the device and may include your daily goal, completed cups, streak, last tracked date, and display preference such as dark mode. The data remains on your device and is used only to support the app experience.

No accounts or cloud services

  • No login or user account is required.
  • No backend/server is used for your hydration data.
  • No Firebase is used.
  • No third-party data sharing occurs.

Ads, notifications, and permissions

  • No ads currently are included.
  • No notifications currently are sent.
  • No sensitive permissions are requested.

Deleting your data

You can delete AquaTrack data by clearing the app data in your device settings or by uninstalling the app. Because the data is stored locally, deleting app data or uninstalling the app may remove your saved progress and settings.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at aquaaatrack@gmail.com.
